# Video Files Directory

Place your video files here for the projection scrims.

Example:
- `ray-allen.mp4` - Ray Allen PE projection video
- `carmelo-anthony.mp4` - Carmelo Anthony PE projection video
- `westbrook.mp4` - Russell Westbrook PE projection video

Video requirements:
- Format: MP4
- Recommended resolution: 1920x1080 or 1280x720
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
